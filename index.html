Timeline

A timeline of my life.

  • 2002-2007

    The Beginning of My Life

    I was born in May 2002, in south central Kentucky. For the next few years, I grew up doing what all kids do; watching cartoons, exploring my yard, and playing with toys. It was at this point in my life that my interest in music revealed itself. I had several drum sets when I was small, and the occasional guitar. Oh - and I was always interested in using the computer.

  • 2007-2013

    Entering School

    I will admit, I was reluctant to begin my schooling. I never liked the idea of being away from my parents or having to stay until 3 everyday. However, that changed soon enough. I still wasn't crazy about going, but I found that most subjects came quite easy to me. Even those that I struggled in, I still seemed to excel at. That took me through my elemetary years, until 2013, when I entered middle school.

  • 2013-2019

    Personal Growth

    When I entered middle school, things began to change. I started to see things and hear things that I was totally oblivious to before. There were cliques in my elementary school, but they were harmless. It wasn't the same here. I quickly realized that some just didn't care about others. I decided that I would not be like that. If someone asked for help, I'd help them. If someone asked for advice, I'd give them advice. And if I coudln't do that, I would at least respond to someone when spoken to.

  • August 2019

    Finally Coding

    That brings us to the current time! In August 2019, I began my senior year of high school and entered the world of coding. It was intimidating at first. The words and numbers on the screen made little sense. When I stared the odd characters within my device, they seemed to stared back at me coldly. But, with a little bit of time, they quickly became comfortable reminders that I was again coding. I started to understand how websites actually worked. It was amazing to see something as simple as an element that I would have had no idea of before! It's hard to believe that I'm creating my own portfolio website using just about a month and a half's worth of knowledge, but I am, and you're reading it!
